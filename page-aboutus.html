our story

Where It
All Began

Fox Global is a family business that began in the year 1992. The inception of Fox Global took place years ago with a restaurant opened by our father. With keen research and productivity over the years, Fox Global became an international venture spread across healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and of course where we began, restaurants. In the past 20+ years, Fox Global has taken its place in the market across, UAE, the Middle East, and South Asia.
